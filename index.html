Prabowo Subianto

Indonesian Politician & National Leader

About

Prabowo Subianto is an Indonesian politician and former military general. He has served as the Minister of Defense of the Republic of Indonesia and is known for his leadership and dedication to national sovereignty.

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Prabowo Subianto Djojohadikusumo
  • Date of Birth: 17 October 1951
  • Place of Birth: Jakarta, Indonesia
  • Nationality: Indonesian

Career Highlights

  • Former Lieutenant General of the Indonesian National Armed Forces
  • Founder and Chairman of Gerindra Party
  • Minister of Defense of Indonesia
